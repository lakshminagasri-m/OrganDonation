Kidney Donation



📊 People Waiting in India:

More than 200,000 people in India are currently in need of a kidney transplant.

🧠 About the Kidney:

The kidneys are two bean-shaped organs located on either side of the spine. They play a vital role in filtering waste, balancing fluids, and producing hormones that regulate blood pressure and red blood cells.

Kidney donation can be from a living or deceased donor. Since humans have two kidneys and can live with one, living donation is common and lifesaving.
